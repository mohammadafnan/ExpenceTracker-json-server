One Solution, Endless Possibilities
Pegasus POS Integration

Seamless Integration with
Foodpanda

Effortlessly connect with Foodpanda to sync orders,
menus, and promotions in real time. Orders placed
through Foodpanda are automatically synced
with your POS system.

Seamless Integration with
Safepay

SafePay is a payment solution in Pakistan
that allows users to make secure online payments.
It enables users to pay for services such as utilities,
mobile top-ups, and online shopping in Pakistan.

Seamless Integration with
Google Map

Integrating Google Maps with Pegasus POS
(Point of Sale) can enhance your system by providing
location-based services like mapping customer
addresses, tracking deliveries, and visualizing routes.

Seamless Integration with
SendGrid

Integrating SendGrid with Pegasus POS
(Point of Sale) can enhance your system by enabling
automated email communications for various
activities like order confirmations, receipts,
promotions, customer support, and more.

Seamless Integration with
SendPK

Integrating SendPK (or any similar service) with
your Pegasus POS system can streamline
communication with customers and enhance the
user experience through automated
notifications and messaging.

Seamless Integration with
FBR (and others SRB, PRA, KPRA)

Integrating with the FBR (Federal Board of Revenue)
ensures that your Pegasus POS system stays compliant with tax regulations, automates the generation of e-invoices, and simplifies tax reporting. your integration with FBR will be efficient.
